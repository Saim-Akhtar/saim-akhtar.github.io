About

I build backend systems where the hard parts live: distributed and event-driven architectures, real-time behavior, and scaling under production load. Over the last few years my work has leaned steadily toward applied AI, building LLM and RAG systems that have to actually hold up in production rather than impress in a demo.

What keeps the work interesting for me is the engineering around the model, not just the model itself: retrieval quality, context management, evaluation, reliability, and the loop between what a system outputs and the real data behind it. That intersection of solid backend foundations and AI-native development is where I want to keep growing.
